VALORANT

game explanation

Valorant is a first-person shooter with an objective-based focus that revolves around planting and diffusing bombs.

You'll be split between attackers and defenders, protecting or assaulting bomb spots, and while your shooting skill is key to success, you'll also be able to use different character abilities.

With some characters focused on disorientating enemies, others focused on support, and some straight out damage-dealers, there's a lot to learn.
